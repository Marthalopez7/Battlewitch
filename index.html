Welcome to BattleWitch! In this game you will throw potions to reveal all of your opponet's witches and cauldrons. Good luck and have fun!

Play Game

How to play

  1. Setup

    2. Before starting the match, setup where you want your characters to be. The witches and cauldrons may not be placed diagonally. Once you are done, press the submit button to start.

  2. Call your shot

    3. Pick a square to possibly uncover a witch or cauldron. Then press the submit button to confirm your selection.

  3. It's a hit!

    4. Choose the space where you might think a witch may be, after confirming your selection, your turn ends.

  4. It’s a miss!

  5. You win!

    6. If you manage to uncover all your opponents witches and cauldrons first, you win the game. You will see a pop-up with a special surprise.

  6. You lose

    7. If your opponent uncovers all your witches and cauldrons first, you lose the game. You will see a pop-up with a “nice” message. ;)
